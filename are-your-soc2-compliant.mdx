title: "Are you SOC2 compliant?"

Yes, Stigg is compliant with both SOC2 Type 2 and ISO27001\. 

These certifications serve as an example to Stigg's maturity and practice of security-by-design, while we ensure that customer data is secure.

More details can be found on [our security portal](https://security.stigg.io/).
