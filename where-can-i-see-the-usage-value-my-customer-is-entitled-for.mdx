---
title: "Where can I see the usage value my customer is entitled for?"
---

You've set a usage limit for a certain feature, let's take seats for example, and you're looking to see how many seats your customer is entitled for.

You can find this information in either one of these places:

#### Subscriptions

Under Customers > Subscription overview, hover over the subscription price and see the price breakdown:

![](/images/e33eaed-image.png)

#### Entitlement usage

Under Customers > Entitlement usage:

![](/images/5217e2e-image.png)

#### Entitlement summary

Under Customers >Entitlement summary:

![](/images/d9a74a3-image.png) 

You can read more about managing customers in Stigg [here](/docs/customers).
