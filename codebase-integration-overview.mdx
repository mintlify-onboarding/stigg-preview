After [your pricing has been modeled in Stigg](modeling-your-pricing), it's time to integrate Stigg's magic into your application.

Stigg's architecture allows engineers to integrate the Stigg SDK to the codebase **only once** when a new feature is added. Changes to the pricing and packaging of the product can then be made by product managers and growth team members without any additional engineering involvement.
